RACES



Restate

Technology has become an integral part of modern society and has had a significant impact on our social lives.

Restate the question or prompt in your own words


Answer

While technology has enabled us to stay connected with others more easily, it has also led to some negative effects on our social lives.

Answer the question or address the prompt


Cite

According to a study by the Pew Research Center, people who use social media frequently tend to feel more isolated and disconnected from their communities.

Cite evidence or examples to support your answer


Explain

Social media can create a false sense of connection and can lead to a lack of genuine social interaction, which can contribute to feelings of isolation and loneliness.

Explain how your evidence supports your answer


Sum-up

Therefore, while technology can be a powerful tool for staying connected with others, it is important to be mindful of its potential negative effects on our social lives.

Sum up your main points and conclude



Technology has become an integral part of modern society and has had a significant impact on our social lives. While technology has enabled us to stay connected with others more easily, it has also led to some negative effects on our social lives. According to a study by the Pew Research Center, people who use social media frequently tend to feel more isolated and disconnected from their communities. Social media can create a false sense of connection and can lead to a lack of genuine social interaction, which can contribute to feelings of isolation and loneliness. Therefore, while technology can be a powerful tool for staying connected with others, it is important to be mindful of its potential negative effects on our social lives.

Point

Infants should not be given iPads, because studies show children under two can face developmental delays if they are exposed to too much screen time.

State your main idea/argument


Evidence/Example

A recent paediatric study showed that infants who are exposed to too much screen time may experience delays in speech development.

Provide evidence supporting your point


Explain

The reason infants are facing these delays is because screen time is replacing other key developmental activities.

Explain how your evidence supports your point


Link

The evidence suggests that infants who have a lot of screen time experience negative consequences in their speech development, and therefore they should not be exposed to iPads at such a young age.

Link to next idea/paragraph or back to your central point



Infants should not be given iPads, because studies show children under two can face developmental delays if they are exposed to too much screen time. A recent paediatric study showed that infants who are exposed to too much screen time may experience delays in speech development. The reason infants are facing these delays is because screen time is replacing other key developmental activities. The evidence suggests that infants who have a lot of screen time experience negative consequences in their speech development, and therefore they should not be exposed to iPads at such a young age.

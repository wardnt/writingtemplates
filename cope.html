About

This website was programmed by me as a design project for school. It was made as a support tool to help students and teachers with their writing tasks. ChatGPT was used to generate a list of common writing methods along with their respective instructions and examples.



Context

Our city is currently experiencing high levels of traffic congestion and air pollution.

Provide background information or context for your argument


Opinion

Building more bike lanes would help to reduce traffic congestion and improve air quality.

Clearly state your opinion or point of view


Proof

Studies have found that cities with robust bike lane networks experience reduced traffic congestion and improved air quality, as well as increased opportunities for physical activity.

Offer evidence and support for your opinion


Ending

By investing in bike lane infrastructure, we can promote a healthier and more sustainable future for our city.

Summarize your argument and conclude with a call to action or final thought



Our city is currently experiencing high levels of traffic congestion and air pollution. Building more bike lanes would help to reduce traffic congestion and improve air quality. Studies have found that cities with robust bike lane networks experience reduced traffic congestion and improved air quality, as well as increased opportunities for physical activity. By investing in bike lane infrastructure, we can promote a healthier and more sustainable future for our city.

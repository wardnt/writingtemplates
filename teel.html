About

This website was programmed by me as a design project for school. It was made as a support tool to help students and teachers with their writing tasks. ChatGPT was used to generate a list of common writing methods along with their respective instructions and examples.



Topic

Education is essential for personal and societal development.

Start with a topic sentence that introduces the main idea of the paragraph


Evidence/Example

According to a report by the United Nations, countries with higher levels of education tend to have higher rates of economic growth and better health outcomes.

Provide evidence or examples to support your topic sentence


Explain

Education helps individuals develop critical thinking skills, which can lead to better decision-making and problem-solving abilities. In turn, this can contribute to improved economic and health outcomes at the societal level.

Explain how your evidence supports your topic sentence


Link

Therefore, it is important to prioritize education in order to promote personal and societal development.

Conclude your paragraph by linking back to your main point



Education is essential for personal and societal development. According to a report by the United Nations, countries with higher levels of education tend to have higher rates of economic growth and better health outcomes. Education helps individuals develop critical thinking skills, which can lead to better decision-making and problem-solving abilities. In turn, this can contribute to improved economic and health outcomes at the societal level. Therefore, it is important to prioritize education in order to promote personal and societal development.

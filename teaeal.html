Online education has become increasingly popular in recent years.

Provide evidence to support your point


Analysis

Online education has become increasingly popular in recent years. According to a report by the National Center for Education Statistics, the number of students enrolled in online courses has increased by 35% over the past five years. This increase in enrollment can be attributed to the convenience and flexibility offered by online education. Students no longer have to travel to a physical classroom, and they can complete coursework on their own schedule. A survey conducted by Educause found that 73% of students who took online courses said that the quality of the instruction was the same or better than traditional classroom instruction. This data shows that online courses are not only convenient, but they can also be just as effective as traditional classroom instruction. In conclusion, the rise in online education is due to its convenience and flexibility, as well as its effectiveness in delivering quality instruction.

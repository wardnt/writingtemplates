Students should be required to wear uniforms in school to promote a sense of unity and discipline. According to a study published in the Journal of Educational Research, schools that require uniforms have lower rates of absenteeism and disciplinary issues. When students wear uniforms, it creates a sense of belonging and unity within the school community. This can lead to a reduction in absenteeism and disciplinary issues because students feel more connected to their school and are less likely to engage in disruptive behavior. In summary, requiring students to wear uniforms in school can promote a sense of unity, reduce absenteeism and disciplinary issues, and ultimately create a more positive and inclusive school environment.

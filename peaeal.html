About

This website was programmed by me as a design project for school. It was made as a support tool to help students and teachers with their writing tasks. ChatGPT was used to generate a list of common writing methods along with their respective instructions and examples.



Point

The internet has had a profound impact on the way we communicate.

State your main idea/argument

The internet has had a profound impact on the way we communicate. According to a recent survey by Pew Research Center, 77% of American adults own a smartphone, and 81% of them use it to access the internet. This statistic shows that the internet has become a ubiquitous part of our lives, with most people using it to communicate. In addition, social media platforms such as Facebook and Twitter have become major tools for communication, with billions of people using them worldwide. This demonstrates that the internet has not only changed the way we communicate, but also created new forms of communication that were not possible before. Therefore, it is clear that the internet has revolutionized the way we communicate, making it faster, easier, and more accessible than ever before.
